Biography

An eminent person with boldness in his eyes, dauntlessness in voice, a spark in thoughts & a heart owned to the mother India, Rohit Sardana was an outstanding Journalist & News Anchor. A passionate boy born on the divine lands of Kurukshetra, with love for his country & the dream in his eyes to create his own identity among the people, holding hands with sincerity, dedication, charm, and the attitude of making the most out of all that comes, Sardana succeeded in becoming the number one TV show Anchor & gracefully hosted Dangal on Aaj Tak & Taal Thok Ke on Zee News.

Married to Ms. Pramila Dixit & had 2 beautiful little Angels Nandika & Kashi, Rohit led a joyous & peaceful life. Such an eminent person whose talent was recognized by various eminent Personalities including Indian Former Cricketer. Kapil Dev went into the skies during the Covid-19 outburst in April 2021 while sincerely obeying his duties on ground zero, creating a void in the hearts of millions.
